---
policy_id: POL-APPAREL-WINDOW
category: apparel
payment_mode: any
issue_type: wrong_size, return_request, changed_mind, quality_complaint
rule_type: window
title: Apparel return window
---
Apparel items are eligible for return within 7 days of delivery. Refunds are issued to the
original payment method by default for any eligible return.

For a size or fit issue, an exchange with a size guide is the recommended first resolution
because it keeps the customer in the product while avoiding a full reverse-logistics return.
A standard refund remains available to the customer where the return is otherwise eligible.
===

policy_id: POL-FOOTWEAR-WINDOW
category: footwear
payment_mode: any
issue_type: wrong_size, return_request, damaged_item
rule_type: window
title: Footwear return window
---
Footwear may be returned within 7 days of delivery. Exchange is supported for size or fit
issues; refunds for eligible returns go to the original payment method by default.
===
---
policy_id: POL-ELECTRONICS-WINDOW
category: electronics
payment_mode: any
issue_type: damaged_item, wrong_item, quality_complaint, return_request
rule_type: window
title: Electronics return window
---
Electronics may be returned within 10 days of delivery. Defective or dead-on-arrival units
qualify for an expedited replacement or a full refund irrespective of the standard window.
Exchange for an identical replacement is the preferred remedy for a confirmed defect.
===

category: home
payment_mode: any
issue_type: return_request, damaged_item, late_delivery
rule_type: window
title: Home & living return window
---
Home and living items may be returned within 7 days of delivery. Damaged-in-transit items
are eligible for replacement or refund regardless of category preferences.

---
policy_id: POL-BOOKS-WINDOW
category: books
payment_mode: any
issue_type: return_request, damaged_item
rule_type: window
title: Books return window
---
Books may be returned within 5 days of delivery if unused and undamaged. Exchange is not
offered for books; an eligible return is refunded to the original payment method.

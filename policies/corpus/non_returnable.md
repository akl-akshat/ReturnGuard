title: Beauty & personal care — non-returnable
---
Beauty and personal-care items are non-returnable once delivered, for hygiene and safety
reasons. This applies whether the order was prepaid or cash on delivery. A return cannot be
accepted simply because the customer changed their mind.

The only exception is an item delivered defective, damaged, expired, or incorrect: such
cases are always eligible for replacement or a full refund.
===
---
policy_id: POL-INNERWEAR-NONRET
category: innerwear
payment_mode: any
issue_type: return_request, wrong_size, changed_mind
rule_type: non_returnable
title: Innerwear — non-returnable
---
Innerwear is non-returnable for hygiene reasons. Size or fit dissatisfaction does not make
the item returnable. A defective or wrong item delivered is the only basis for replacement
or refund.
===
---
policy_id: POL-GROCERY-NONRET
category: grocery
payment_mode: any
issue_type: return_request, damaged_item, missing_item, quality_complaint
rule_type: non_returnable
title: Grocery & perishables — non-returnable
---
Grocery and perishable goods are non-returnable. However, items delivered damaged, spoiled,
expired, missing, or incorrect are eligible for a refund or replacement on report.

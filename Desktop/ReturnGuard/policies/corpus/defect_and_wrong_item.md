title: Defect, damage & wrong-item exception (overrides window and non-returnable)
---
An item delivered defective, damaged, dead-on-arrival, expired, incorrect, or missing is
always eligible for a remedy, regardless of category or return window. This exception
overrides both the standard return window and any non-returnable-category rule.

The appropriate remedy for a genuine defect or a wrong item shipped is an expedited
replacement or a full refund to the original payment method, optionally with a small
goodwill credit. A denial is never appropriate for a genuine defect or wrong-item case;
the customer must not be left out of pocket for a platform or product fault.

Because a wrong item shipped is a platform error, the resolution should be
customer-favourable and should not require the customer to bear return friction.

category: "*"

issue_type: refund_status, return_request, damaged_item, wrong_item
rule_type: refund_mode
title: COD refund mode — bank transfer by default
---
For cash-on-delivery orders, an eligible refund is paid to the customer's bank account,
which is the original-method equivalent for a COD purchase. The customer is always entitled
to this standard refund where the return is eligible.

Store credit may be offered only as an optional, incentivised alternative — for example a
slightly higher credit value — but it must never be presented as the only option for a
genuine refund. Defaulting a genuine refund to store-credit-only is prohibited.
===
---
policy_id: POL-PREPAID-REFUND-MODE
category: "*"
payment_mode: PREPAID
issue_type: refund_status, return_request, damaged_item, wrong_item
rule_type: refund_mode
title: Prepaid refund mode — original instrument by default
---
For prepaid orders, an eligible refund is returned to the original payment instrument used
at checkout. This standard refund is always available to the customer for an eligible return.

Store credit or a retention coupon may be offered as an optional incentive alongside the
standard refund, never as a mandatory substitute for it.
